EVENTS

Ladies and gentlemen,

Today marks a significant milestone in the journey of our TechNurtue 1.0 students. It is with great pride and joy that we gather here to celebrate the completion of this remarkable free tech training program. On behalf of Bems Institute of Technology and Vocational Studies, I extend my warmest congratulations to each and every one of our students.

TechNurtue 1.0 was born out of a vision to bridge the ICT knowledge gap and empower our youth with essential digital skills. We recognized the importance of equipping our young generation with the tools they need to succeed in an increasingly digital world. And now, as we come to the end of this program, we can proudly say that together, we have made significant strides in achieving that goal. Throughout this journey, our students have displayed unwavering commitment, dedication, and a thirst for knowledge.

You have embraced every challenge, overcome obstacles, and consistently pushed yourselves to reach new heights of understanding. Your resilience and enthusiasm have been truly inspiring to witness. TechNurtue 1.0 has provided you with a solid foundation in various areas of technology, enabling you to explore new horizons and pursue your passions. From coding and web development to data analysis and Product Design, you have gained invaluable skills that will open doors to countless opportunities. But this program was never just about acquiring technical skills. It was about nurturing your growth as individuals, fostering teamwork, and instilling a sense of purpose. And I am confident that it has succeeded in doing just that.

You have not only become proficient in the latest technologies, but you have also developed critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills that are vital for success in any field. As you move forward from this moment, remember that the journey does not end here.

TechNurtue 1.0 has laid the foundation, but it is up to you to build upon it. Embrace every opportunity that comes your way, continue to challenge yourselves, and never stop learning. The world of technology is ever-evolving, and it is those who adapt and evolve with it that will thrive.

I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude to the Bems Institute of Technology and Vocational Studies for their unwavering support and dedication to this program. Without their vision and commitment, none of this would have been possible.

I also extend my appreciation to the instructors and mentors who have selflessly shared their knowledge and expertise, guiding our students every step of the way. Finally, to our TechNurtue 1.0 students, I want to say how immensely proud I am of each and every one of you. You have proven that with determination, hard work, and a thirst for knowledge, anything is possible.

Remember that you are now becoming part of a community of technologists and innovators who are shaping the world. Embrace your role as change-makers, and let your achievements inspire others to follow in your footsteps. As you embark on this new chapter, I encourage you to dream big, push boundaries, and never doubt your ability to make a difference.

Congratulations once again, and may your journey be filled with endless success, fulfillment, and happiness. As you now become Fellows of the BemsTech Club. I look forward to your final induction into the highly sought-after Bems Developer Hub Thank you. Dr. Pastra Arinze Etie. Program Founder.
